Joseph Smith Jr.

Emma Hale Smith
Joseph and Emma Smith married on January 18, 1827 in South Bainbridge, New York.

Of his marriage, in 1842, Joseph said,
"What transports of joy swelled my bosom, when I took by the hand on that night, my beloved Emma[,] . . . even the wife of my youth: and the choice of my heart."
